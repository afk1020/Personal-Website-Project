Puppy Phase

Dakota is a field Labrador Retriever. She was born on June 9th 2016 and was 1 of 3 females in a litter of 9 puppies. When picking out a puppy, she was the friendliest and gave many kisses.

Post-Puppy Phase

Dakota now weighs 75lbs, but still is very friendly. She is now almost 5 years old. She loves to go on walks and take naps on the couch.
